Disease Library

Rose Plant Conditions

Our AI detects these 5 conditions from a single leaf photo

✅

Healthy Leaf

No Disease

Vibrant green leaf with no spots, holes, or discoloration. Your plant is thriving and healthy.

Tip: Water at the base in the morning and check leaves weekly.
🍄

Black Spot

Fungal

Dark circular spots on leaves caused by the fungus Diplocarpon rosae. Thrives in warm, wet conditions.

Fix: Fungicide spray + remove infected leaves immediately.
💧

Downy Mildew

Water Mold

Yellow patches on leaf tops with grey fuzzy growth underneath. Spreads fast in cool, humid weather.

Fix: Copper fungicide + improve airflow around plant.
🦠

Rose Rust

Fungal

Orange powdery pustules on the underside of leaves. Spreads quickly in warm and humid conditions.

Fix: Sulfur-based fungicide spray every week.
🐛

Rose Sawfly

Insect

Tiny larvae skeletonize leaves leaving see-through patches. Usually found on the underside of leaves.

Fix: Neem oil spray + manually remove visible larvae.
